Philippines!

The Philippines, an archipelago in Southeast Asia, is a vibrant and diverse nation composed of over 7,000 islands. Known for its stunning landscapes, including pristine beaches, lush rainforests, and volcanic peaks, the country offers a rich variety of cultural heritage influenced by centuries of Spanish and American colonization.

Here are the top 3 best locations if you visit the Philippines!

Palawan!

  • Breathtaking Underground River
  • Stunning Coral Reefs and Dive Sites
  • El Nido's Spectacular Islands
  • Cultural Richness of the Indigenous Communities
  • Palawan's Longest White Sand Beach

Bohol!

  • Famous Chocolate Hills
  • Home to one of the Tarsier Conservation Area
  • Historic Baclayon Church
  • Loboc River Cruise
  • Man-made Forest

Tagaytay!

  • Scenic Famous Taal Volcano and Lakes
  • Cooler Climate and Highland Setting
  • Sky Ranch and People's Park in the Sky
  • Famous for the popular food Bulalo
  • Zipline Adventures
